# Contact & Support Hours

## Support Channels
- **Live Chat:** Available on our website, 24/7
- **Email:** support@techstore.com — response within 4 hours during business hours
- **Phone:** 1-800-TECH-HELP — available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

## Business Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM EST
- Sunday: Closed
- Holiday hours may vary — check our website for updates

## Escalation
- If your issue is not resolved within 24 hours, it will be automatically escalated
- You can request to speak with a supervisor at any time
- For urgent issues (security, billing errors), call our priority line: 1-800-TECH-911
