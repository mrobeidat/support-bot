# Pricing & Discounts

## Price Match Guarantee
- We match any competitor's price within 14 days of purchase
- The item must be identical (same brand, model, condition)
- Contact support with a link to the competitor's listing

## Discount Programs
- **Student Discount:** 15% off with a valid .edu email
- **Military Discount:** 20% off with military ID verification
- **Loyalty Program:** Earn 1 point per $1 spent. 100 points = $5 off

## Sales & Promotions
- Seasonal sales happen quarterly (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)
- Newsletter subscribers get early access to sales
- Flash sales are announced on our social media channels

## Bulk Orders
- Orders of 10+ units qualify for a 10% bulk discount
- Orders of 50+ units qualify for a 20% bulk discount
- Contact our sales team for custom bulk pricing
