# Account & Security

## Creating an Account
- Visit our website and click "Sign Up"
- You can sign up with email or Google/Apple SSO
- A verification email will be sent to confirm your account

## Password Reset
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 24 hours)
4. Create a new password (minimum 8 characters, must include a number)

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for account security
- Supported methods: SMS, authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
- Go to Account Settings → Security → Enable 2FA

## Deleting Your Account
- Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account
- All personal data will be removed within 30 days
- Active orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion
- This action is irreversible
