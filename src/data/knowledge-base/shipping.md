# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
- Free shipping on all orders over $50
- Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days
- Tracking number provided via email once order ships

## Express Shipping
- Express shipping costs $12.99
- Delivery within 2-3 business days
- Available for all domestic orders

## International Shipping
- International shipping starts at $24.99
- Delivery takes 10-15 business days
- Customs fees and duties are the responsibility of the customer
- We currently ship to US, Canada, UK, and EU countries

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST are processed the same day
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST are processed the next business day
- You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships
